# Digital Identity

Dutch and European digital identity frameworks.

## Dutch Identity

| System | Level | Use |
|--------|-------|-----|
| DigiD | Basis | Citizen login |
| eHerkenning | Midden | Business login |

## EUDI Wallet (Regulation 2024/1183)

- PID (Person Identification Data)
- Attestations (diplomas, licenses)
- Qualified Electronic Signatures
- Selective Disclosure
- Cross-border recognition
