# EU AI Act Compliance

EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689) compliance for Dutch government.

## Risk Classification

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Unacceptable | Prohibited practices |
| High | Annex III use cases |
| Limited | Direct interaction |
| Minimal | Low risk |

## High-Risk Requirements

- Risk management system (Art. 9)
- Data governance (Art. 10)
- Technical documentation (Art. 11)
- Transparency (Art. 13)
- Human oversight (Art. 14)

## Penalties

| Violation | Maximum |
|-----------|---------|
| Prohibited practices | 35M EUR or 7% turnover |
| Other infringements | 15M EUR or 3% turnover |
